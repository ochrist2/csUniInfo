Picking an Ontario University for Computer Science (2016)

Some thoughts to consider:



Although picking a school purely based on its academic reputation isn't a bad thing to do, it is important to consider other factors when deciding where you want to attend school for 4-5 years of your life. Whether you want to do co-op, want to work in a certain city, want an "authentic" university experience, want an exceptional student life, want to attend somewhere for the prestige, and whether you just like the school are some examples of things to keep in mind before you decide. With this in mind, the following are the best choices for computer science when applying to an Ontario university in 2017.
